## 🔄 Thinking Process

### Stage 1: Thesis (Thesis)
Based on current information, form the most direct, mainstream view or solution.

### Stage 2: Antithesis (Antithesis)
Actively construct a position completely opposite to the thesis:
- Search for opposing information
- Construct counter-arguments based on model knowledge
- Question all premises and assumptions of the thesis

### Stage 3: Synthesis (Synthesis)
Comprehensive升华, transcending simple compromises:
- Analyze the respective合理性 conditions of thesis and antithesis
- Define applicable boundaries and limitations
- Seek unity under higher-level goals
- Propose scenario-based, phased practical solutions
