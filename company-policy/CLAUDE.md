### 01. Attendance System (考勤制度)

#### Multi-system Parallel Usage

| System | Purpose | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **elink** | Daily check-in (上下班打卡) | **Only basis for settlement** |
| **Face Recognition** | Daily check-in (门禁刷脸) | Will be included in attendance settlement |
| **DingTalk** | Daily check-in | Auxiliary record only, not for settlement |
| **Tianyang APP** | Daily check-in | Internal record |

**Important**: Must complete two check-ins daily (clock in and clock out) and fill in work hours and report work in time.

#### Check-in Requirements

**Time Norms**:
- System default: 9:00-17:00 (not actual attendance time)
- Follow project team requirements for actual check-in times
- Late/early departure (even 1 minute) is a violation

**Location Norms**:
- Strictly prohibited to check in on the road
- Must report location anomalies immediately
- For going out, apply via elink first

**Photo Evidence**:
- Take photo immediately after each face recognition (earliest clock-in, latest clock-out)
- Ensure photo clearly shows time
- Photos serve as appeal evidence if attendance data is abnormal

#### Work Hours Reporting (工时填报)

**elink Work Hours Guidelines**:
1. Work log divided into morning and afternoon, fill after clock-out
2. Content should support daily workload, detailed and quantified (15-100 characters)
3. Cannot be identical for a whole week, long-term items should show quantified indicators
4. Use "配合" (assist) or "协助" (support) instead of "负责" (responsible)
5. Avoid sensitive information: procurement, project initiation, feasibility study, party building, recruitment
6. Leave/vacation: do not fill work log (leave blank, don't write anything)
7. Weekly work hours can be modified within the week, deadline: Friday 12:00 PM
8. Other work hours need project manager to return for modification

**Abnormal Work Hours**:
| Situation | Handling |
|-----------|----------|
| Forgot check-in | Upload approved supplementary check-in record + work evidence |
| Half-day leave | No evidence needed, note morning/afternoon leave in reason |
| Late/Early departure | Half-day work hours not approved |
| Work hours not filled | No settlement for that day |

**System Lock Times**:
- **elink work hours**: Locked on 15th of following month
- **elink supplementary check-in**: Locked on 10th of following month (max 6 times/month)
- **Tianyang APP supplementary check-in**: Must be within 3 working days
- **Weekly work hours**: Locked Friday 10:00 PM

#### Supplementary Check-in (补卡)

**Limits**:
- **elink**: Max 6 times/month, deadline: 10th of following month
- **Tianyang APP**: Within 3 working days (including the day), otherwise appeal process (3 times/year)

**Requirements**:
- Fill actual attendance time (don't use system default)
- Supplementary clock-in: before 8:30, clock-out: after 17:30
- Work duration must be 8 hours, otherwise calculated as half day
- Witness: choose colleague from same team (not yourself)
- Approver: Tianyang project manager

**Evidence Required**:
- Account not given: door photo (with date, time, face) or DingTalk record
- Normal forgot check-in: elink work discussion screenshot or DingTalk record
- Overtime cannot be supplemented

**Anomaly Handling** (location drift, system bug, door malfunction):
- Within 10 minutes: DingTalk check-in + Tianyang check-in + door photo + elink work communication screenshot
- Send all 4 proofs to Tianyang project assistant for filing
- Naming format: Name + Date + Anomaly Type (e.g., "张三0529定位漂移")

#### Leave Process (请假流程)

**Three Steps**:
1. Get approval from China Southern Power Grid project leader
2. Submit leave application in elink
3. Submit leave application in Tianyang APP (Workbench → Attendance Leave → Apply for Leave)

**Notes**:
- Must apply in advance, cannot be retroactive
- Requires Tianyang responsible person approval
- After approval, cannot withdraw or cancel
- No need to fill work hours on leave day
- Must sync to China Southern Power Grid project manager via elink or email
- Leave minimum unit: 0.5 day
- Morning leave, return afternoon: must clock in before 13:30
- Leave 3+ days: must sync to China Southern Power Grid department head via elink or email

**Half-day Leave**:
- Must complete clock-in and clock-out in elink and Tianyang APP
- If afternoon leave: must clock out when leaving at noon
- Forgot check-in or system failure: must supplement

**Cancel Leave (销假)**:
- Submit cancel leave application for approval
- Must report to project manager offline and get approval

#### Going Out (外出)

- Apply via elink (select "外出" type)
- No location restriction for check-in
- Follow unit requirements for check-in

#### Overtime (加班)

**Two Required Steps** (both required):
1. China Southern Power Grid department submits overtime application before overtime starts, approval completed
2. Employee checks in according to actual time in elink (supplementary check-in invalid)

**Notes**:
- Weekday overtime starts from 19:00, minimum 1 hour unit
- Overtime application must be completed before overtime starts
- Default deduction: 12:00-13:30 (lunch), 17:30-19:00 (dinner)
- Must have clock-in and clock-out times, supplementary check-in invalid
- No need to fill separate work log for overtime
- Weekend/holiday overtime: application must be completed before overtime starts
- Legal holiday overtime: additional email/elink request to department head
- Morning forgot check-in: must complete supplementary check-in before overtime starts
- Morning leave: must clock in via elink and door before noon for overtime to count
- Shift duty: no fixed time, check-in based on project shift settings, supports supplementary check-in but not going out/leave

**Overtime Anomaly**:
- Check if both steps completed
- If still no data: contact China Southern Power Grid department assistant (not Tianyang personnel)
- Tianyang only uses final settlement sheet for overtime pay

#### Business Trip (出差)

**Before Trip**:
1. Contact Tianyang project assistant for process consultation
2. China Southern Power Grid submits trip application (elink or email)
3. Trip confirmation form scan (ensure complete seals)

**During Trip**:
1. Accommodation proof (invoice + check-in list) - Invoice header: check Tianyang APP → My → Invoice Info
2. Inter-city transportation proof (electronic version, don't print)

**Note**: Hotel and transportation costs are self-paid, must get proper invoices and itineraries

**After Trip**:
1. Submit application, confirmation form, invoices, itineraries to Tianyang project assistant
2. Reimburse via Tianyang OA
3. Learning portal: Tianyang APP → Workbench → Tianyang Academy → Knowledge Base → Search "财务报销&贴票流程"

#### Salary Settlement (薪资结算)

- **Payday**: 15th of each month
- **Basis**: Tianyang OA work report data (excluding current month overtime pay)
- **Reconciliation**: Company reviews attendance data after receiving China Southern Power Grid settlement sheet
- **Adjustment**: Overtime paid when settlement sheet received; deductions for attendance anomalies
- **Note**: Attendance records affect long-term settlement, not one-time; anomalies may cause salary adjustments months later

### 02. Behavior Norms & Information Security (行为规范与信息安全)

#### Confidentiality System (保密制度)

**Core Responsibilities**:
1. Bear information security responsibility within scope of duties
2. Ensure confidentiality, integrity, availability of all information accessed

**Information Operation Norms**:
1. Do not illegally record, store, copy, or transmit secret information
2. Do not open internal systems (including China Southern Power Grid logos or internal data) to the internet without authorization
3. Test data must be desensitized, strictly prohibit using real data (unless authorized)

**Content Publishing & Speech Discipline**:
1. Do not publish or leak company business secrets or operational information
2. Do not disseminate information or speech that may negatively impact the company
3. Do not publish any company information externally without authorization

**Emergency & Reporting Mechanism**:
1. Report immediately when information security incident occurs or may occur
2. Take emergency measures as required, preserve original records
3. Cooperate with investigation and implement rectification requirements

#### Network Security Regulations (网络制度)

**Terminal & Software Security**:
1. Strictly prohibit empty or weak passwords
2. Strictly prohibit installing unauthorized software from unknown sources (QQ, Weibo, Douyin, etc.)
3. Do not close or uninstall antivirus, anti-leakage and other security control software
4. Strictly follow company terminal management and network access norms

**Account Password & Information Protection**:
1. Strictly prohibit empty or weak passwords
2. Do not post plain text passwords or complete IP addresses in exposed areas
3. Must lock screen when leaving workstation, encrypt important files
4. Browser should not save account passwords, clear browsing history regularly

**Network Behavior & Content Control**:
1. Strictly prohibit publishing company information to internet public platforms
2. Strictly prohibit setting up wireless hotspots without authorization
3. Do not bypass or damage various network security control measures
4. Strictly follow company network security regulations

**Emergency Response**:
1. Report suspected network security incidents immediately, preserve the scene
2. Actively cooperate with network security incident investigation
3. Strictly follow company network security regulations

**Important**: Strictly prohibit "one machine dual network" (一机双网)

#### elink Operation Norms

**File Transfer**:
1. Important files must be encrypted before transfer via elink
2. Attachments over 500MB should use hard drive certified by technical department

**Content Compliance**:
- Strictly prohibit posting negative, attacking, political, or sensitive speech in elink groups
- Screenshots and complaints will be treated as serious disciplinary violations

**Account Login**:
- Do not check auto-login in elink
- Must log out when leaving workstation

**Data Retention**:
1. Backend retains data for no more than 3 months
2. Regularly clean historical data and cache files

#### Service Behavior Standards (服务行为规范)

**Badge & Office Discipline**:
1. Strictly prohibit forging, altering, stealing or unauthorized lending of badges
2. Must wear badge properly after entering
3. Enter/exit with badge/temporary badge, do not tailgate others
4. Strictly follow venue safety management regulations
5. Provide services strictly according to service time period, no late arrival or early departure

**Dress & Appearance**:
1. Dress neatly and appropriately, no tank tops, sleeveless shirts, mini skirts, exaggerated clothing
2. No exaggerated hair color, strange hairstyles, or exaggerated headwear
3. No slippers, sandals, or crocs in office (except in severe weather)

**Office Behavior**:
1. Work in designated office area, do not wander between posts or buildings, do not leave post without authorization
2. Do not engage in non-work activities during office hours (gaming, shopping, reading novels, etc.)
3. Turn off computer when leaving workstation

**Environment & Safety Awareness**:
1. Actively maintain office area hygiene, keep items organized
2. Smoking strictly prohibited in office area
3. Maintain environmental cleanliness
4. Last person to leave responsible for turning off lights and closing windows

### 03. Common Issues & Violation Cases (常见问题与违规案例)

#### Top 5 High-frequency Violation Scenarios

**Attendance Management Violations**:
1. **Forgot check-in, forgot work hours** → Incomplete attendance data, directly affects salary and project settlement
2. **Location drift not reported** → Causes attendance data anomaly, triggers audit risk
3. **Going out without application** → Check-in at non-resident location requires advance application, otherwise affects settlement process and data validity

**Information Security Violations**:
1. **Installing unauthorized software** → Violates security regulations, may cause data leakage, system failure, or virus intrusion
2. **Leaking screen information** → Any form of sensitive information leakage will face serious handling, including legal accountability

#### Violation Cost Calculator

| Type | Consequence |
|------|-------------|
| **Attendance Violation** | Deduction by daily wage (late, early departure, missed check-in) |
| **Security Violation** | Contract fine (unauthorized software, one machine dual network) |
| **Image Violation** | Project point deduction (improper dress, poor office discipline) |
